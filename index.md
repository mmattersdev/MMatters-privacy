# Privacy Policy for Money Matters

**Effective date:** _(6 December 2025)_

## Introduction
This app (“Money Matters”) is provided by Mmatters Dev (“we”, “us”). Contact: [mmatters.dev@gmail.com](mailto:mmatters.dev@gmail.com).

## Data Collection & Storage
- Invoices, quotes, client/contact details, attachments (images/PDFs), and app settings are stored locally on your device.
- We do not collect, transmit, or sell personal or financial data to any server.

## Sharing & Exports
- PDF/CSV exports and email sharing are user-initiated.
- Files are only shared to the destinations you choose.

## Permissions
- **Photos/Files**: Used only when you select a logo/header/footer or add attachments (receipts, payment QR). No other media is accessed.

## Tracking
- No tracking. No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

## Security & Retention
- Data remains on your device until you delete it.
- Backups/archives are created only when you explicitly export or archive.

## Your Choices
- You can use the core app without granting photo/file access; only attachment/logo features will be limited.

## Contact
If you have questions, email [mmatters.dev@gmail.com](mailto:mmatters.dev@gmail.com).
